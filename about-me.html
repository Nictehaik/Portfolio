Stages of website development

1 Briefing

At this stage, there is an acquaintance with the costumer and his project, we discuss what tasks need to be solved. Next, we work out the structure of the site, we determine the terms and prices.

2 Analytics

The purpose of this step is to begin to develop a clearly defined product roadmap. Conduct a more in-depth competitive analysis. Think about the difference from other markets to evaluate success once the product is launched.

3 Prototyping

Product documentation and construction. Prototypes, in the initial stages, can be as simple as a drawing or as complex as a computer reproduction of the initial design.

4 Design

During this initial design phase, a meeting is held with project participants to produce a mockup of the product based on the final prototype. The design should be created with a target audience in mind and should be complemented with the key features of the new product.

5 Adaptive

For the product to go to market, it must first be validated, tested and adapted. The development of the concept is generated, it could include the development of a software or the failures of the initial prototype. Interface tests are generated during this stage to detect risks in the development code or bugs that affect the consumer.

6 The Final

In this stage the product development is prepared to launch on your website and start with the ecommerce strategy.
